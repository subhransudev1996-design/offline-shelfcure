ShelfCure Field Sales & Lead Intelligence System — PRD
Product Name

ShelfCure Sales CRM & Field Visit Intelligence System

1. Product Vision

Build a mobile-first pharmacy field sales management system tightly integrated with ShelfCure Admin Panel to help the sales team:

Manage pharmacy leads efficiently
Track physical pharmacy visits
Avoid revisiting wrong areas
Improve follow-up discipline
Increase demo-to-conversion ratio
Improve field productivity
Enable geo-based lead intelligence
Centralize all sales activity in one system

The system will primarily operate through:

Android Mobile App (Field Team)
Existing ShelfCure Web Admin Panel (Monitoring & Management)
